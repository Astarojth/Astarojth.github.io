I am a first-year Ph.D. student in Computer Science, New York University (Courant Institute of Mathematical Sciences; based at NYU Abu Dhabi). My research focuses on Trustworthy AI and Agentic AI. I am passionate about creating a harmony future of human and AI. I also have long term collaborations with researchers in Nanyang Technology University and Zhejiang University. I am actively looking for research collaborations in LLM safety, trustworthy AI, and human-AI interaction.
